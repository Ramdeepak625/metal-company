About Company

Established in the year 1990, Industrial - Connections, Chennai, is amongst the leading manufacturers, suppliers and exporters of a wide collection of die-casted metal accessories for belts, key chains, metal logo, bag logo, lapel pins, logos, name boards, badges, buttons. Apart from these, we are also offering leather fashion and garment accessories. These are manufactured using quality raw material, such as brass, cloth, steel and leather; that are sourced from reliable vendors to ensure the quality of the end products. These mesmerizing accessories are widely appreciated for their adroit designs, different color combinations and striking features. We have with us a well-developed infrastructure that helps in meeting the bulk requirements within stipulated time frame. Moreover, for storing and efficient management of our accessories, we have a capacious warehouse unit that is equipped with all modern amenities and equipment. Moreover, to meet the varied requirements of our clients, we also offer customized facility of our complete range.We are successfully managed under the able guidance of our chairman, Mr. S. Elamathi, who has an immense industry experience and in-depth knowledge that enabled us to carve a niche for ourselves in the industry.
